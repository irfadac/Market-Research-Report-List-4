<p><p>Tablet Compression Machines are categorized into two main types: Single Station Type and Multiple Station Type. The Single Station Type machines have only one station for compressing tablets at a time, making them suitable for small scale production. On the other hand, Multiple Station Type machines have multiple stations that enable simultaneous compression of multiple tablets, making them ideal for high volume production. Each type caters to different production needs and requirements, offering versatility and efficiency in tablet manufacturing processes.</p></p>
