<p><p>Butt Weld Reducers are pipe fittings that connect pipes of different diameters. Two main types are available in the market - Concentric and Eccentric Reducers. Concentric Reducers have a symmetrical design with a centerline at the meeting point of the two pipes, providing uniform flow. Eccentric Reducers, on the other hand, have an off-center design, making them suitable for applications where the pipes need to maintain a specific level or drainage flow. Both types are widely used in various industries for joining pipes of different sizes.</p></p>
