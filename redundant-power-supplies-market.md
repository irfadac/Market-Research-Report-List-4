<p><strong>The Redundant Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1+1 Power Configuration</li><li>2+1 Power Configuration</li><li>2+2 Power Configuration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Redundant power supplies offer different configurations to ensure continuous power supply and prevent downtime in critical systems. The 1+1 power configuration provides redundancy with one backup power supply for each primary supply. The 2+1 power configuration offers two primary power supplies and one backup to increase reliability. The 2+2 power configuration provides dual redundancy with two backup power supplies for each primary supply, offering the highest level of reliability and fault tolerance in mission-critical applications.</p></p>
