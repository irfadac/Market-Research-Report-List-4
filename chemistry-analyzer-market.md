<p><p>Chemistry analyzers can be classified into two main types based on their physical size and configuration: floor-standing and benchtop. Floor-standing analyzers are larger in size and are designed for high-volume testing in larger laboratories. They typically have a higher throughput capacity and can accommodate a wider range of tests. On the other hand, benchtop analyzers are smaller and more compact, making them suitable for smaller laboratories or clinics with limited space. They are generally more affordable and easier to operate, but may have a lower throughput capacity compared to floor-standing analyzers.</p></p>
