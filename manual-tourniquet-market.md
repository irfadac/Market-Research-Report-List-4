<p><p>Manual tourniquets are available in different types such as Latex Tourniquet and TPE Tourniquet. Latex tourniquets are made from natural rubber latex material, while TPE tourniquets are made from thermoplastic elastomers. Both types of tourniquets are widely used in medical settings for temporarily stopping the blood flow to a limb during medical procedures. Latex tourniquets are known for their durability and elasticity, while TPE tourniquets are popular for being latex-free and hypoallergenic, making them suitable for patients with latex allergies.</p></p>
