<p><p>Degasifiers, such as Packed Towers and Tray Towers, are used in various industries to remove impurities from liquids by stripping gases. Packed Towers consist of a column filled with packing material that increases surface area for gas-liquid contact, while Tray Towers use trays to facilitate gas-liquid separation. Both market types offer efficient degasification solutions for different applications, with Packed Towers being more common in industries such as chemical processing and wastewater treatment, while Tray Towers are often preferred in oil and gas refining.</p></p>
