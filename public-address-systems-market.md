<p><p>Public address systems are divided into two main market types: portable systems and fixed systems. On the other hand, fixed systems are permanently installed in buildings, stadiums, airports, and other facilities. They offer higher power output and better sound quality, making them ideal for large venues that require consistent and reliable audio coverage. Both types of systems cater to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
