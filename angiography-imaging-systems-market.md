<p><strong>The Angiography Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography</li><li>CT Angiography</li><li>MRI Angiography</li><li>MR-CT Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Angiography imaging systems encompass various modalities for visualizing blood vessels and diagnosing vascular conditions. X-Ray angiography uses fluoroscopy to provide real-time images of blood flow. CT angiography combines X-ray and computer technology for detailed vascular images, often facilitating faster diagnoses. MRI angiography utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves to visualize blood vessels non-invasively. MR-CT angiography integrates both MRI and CT techniques, enhancing detail and accuracy in vascular imaging, thus supporting comprehensive patient assessment and treatment planning.</p></p>
