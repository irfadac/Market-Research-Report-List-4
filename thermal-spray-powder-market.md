<p><p>Thermal spray powders are categorized into two main types: ceramic powder and metal/alloy powder. Ceramic powders are composed of inorganic materials such as oxides, carbides, and nitrides, providing properties like wear resistance and thermal insulation. On the other hand, metal/alloy powders consist of metals and their alloys, offering characteristics such as corrosion resistance and improved mechanical strength. These powders are utilized in various industries for coating applications to enhance the surface properties of materials and extend their lifespan.</p></p>
