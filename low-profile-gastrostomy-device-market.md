<p><p>The Low Profile Gastrostomy Device market comprises two main types - obturated and non-obturated devices. </p><p>Obturated devices have a balloon or internal bumper to secure them in place, which helps reduce the risk of dislodgement. They are typically favored for long-term use. </p><p>Non-obturated devices do not have a balloon or internal bumper, relying on external support like a dressing or skin disc. They are often used for short-term feeding or medication administration. Both types cater to different patient needs and preferences in the gastrostomy device market.</p></p>
