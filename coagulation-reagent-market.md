<p><p>Coagulation reagents are essential for performing tests like PT (prothrombin time), APTT (activated partial thromboplastin time), and FIB (fibrinogen). PT tests the function of the extrinsic and common coagulation pathways, while APTT assesses the intrinsic and common pathways. FIB test evaluates the level of fibrinogen, a crucial protein for blood clotting. These tests help diagnose and monitor conditions like bleeding disorders, liver diseases, and monitoring anticoagulant therapy.
