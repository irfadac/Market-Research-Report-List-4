<p><p>The upper arm blood pressure monitor market comprises two main types: mercury blood pressure monitors and digital blood pressure monitors. Mercury monitors utilize a column of mercury to measure blood pressure, providing accurate readings but raising concerns over mercury exposure. Digital monitors employ sensors and electronic components, offering convenience, ease of use, and automatic readings. While digital models are increasingly popular due to their user-friendly features, mercury monitors are gradually being phased out due to health and safety regulations.</p></p>
