<p><p>Under-screen Fingerprint Technology Market Types include Optical Fingerprint Recognition and Ultrasonic Fingerprint Recognition. Optical Fingerprint Recognition uses light to capture a 2D image of the fingerprint, while Ultrasonic Fingerprint Recognition uses sound waves to create a 3D image. Optical recognition is more common and cheaper, but can be less secure than ultrasonic recognition, which offers higher accuracy and reliability. Both technologies are used in smartphones and other devices to provide convenient and secure biometric authentication.</p></p>
