<p><p>The Bone Densitometer market is categorized into various types based on the technology used for measuring bone density. Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) is the most widely used method for bone density measurement. Ultrasound technology is also used for evaluating bone health. Quantitative computed tomography (QCT) provides detailed information about bone density and structure. Other types of bone densitometers include single-energy X-ray absorptiometry (SXA) and peripheral dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (pDEXA). These technologies help in diagnosing osteoporosis and monitoring bone health.</p></p>
