<p><p>Hot Strip Mill and Cold Rolling Mill are two types of rolling mills used in the metalworking industry. </p><p>Hot Strip Mill is used for rolling metal strips that are heated beyond their recrystallization temperature. This process helps to reduce the thickness of the metal strip and improve its mechanical properties. Hot Strip Mill is commonly used in the production of steel sheets and coils.</p><p>On the other hand, Cold Rolling Mill is used for rolling metal strips at room temperature or below the recrystallization temperature. Cold Rolling Mill helps to improve the surface finish, dimensional accuracy, and mechanical properties of the metal strip. Cold-rolled products are commonly used in industries like automotive, construction, and appliances.</p><p>The Hot Strip Mill and Cold Rolling Mill Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.
