<p><ul><li>Optoelectronic Isolated Amplifiers</li><li>Capacitor Isolated Amplifiers</li><li>Transformer Isolated Amplifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The isolated amplifier market encompasses various types, including optoelectronic, capacitor, and transformer isolated amplifiers. Optoelectronic isolated amplifiers utilize light for signal transmission, ensuring high-speed data transfer and electrical isolation. Capacitor isolated amplifiers employ capacitive coupling to achieve isolation, offering benefits in size and performance. Transformer isolated amplifiers use magnetic coupling for signal isolation, known for their robustness and ability to handle high power levels. Each type serves specific applications, ensuring safety and signal integrity in diverse electronic environments.</p></p>
