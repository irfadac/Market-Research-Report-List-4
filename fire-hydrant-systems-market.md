<p><p>Fire hydrant systems are essential for fire safety and come in two main types: wet barrel and dry barrel systems. Wet barrel systems are commonly used in regions with mild climates, as they are constantly filled with water and ready for immediate use. Dry barrel systems, on the other hand, are more suitable for cold climates where water in the hydrant might freeze. These systems are designed to only fill with water when needed, preventing freezing and damage in colder temperatures.</p></p>
