<p><p>Insulated wall panels are divided into two main types: Polyurethane Insulation Panels and XPS Insulation Panels. Polyurethane Insulation Panels are known for their high insulation properties and energy efficiency. They are commonly used in residential and commercial buildings. XPS Insulation Panels, on the other hand, are known for their moisture resistance and durability. They are often used in applications where moisture exposure is a concern, such as basements or underground structures. Both types of insulated wall panels provide effective thermal insulation and help improve energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
