<p><ul><li>Structural UV Adhesive</li><li>Sealant Adhesion UV Adhesive</li><li>Pressure Sensitive UV Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV adhesives are classified into three main types: structural UV adhesives, sealant adhesion UV adhesives, and pressure-sensitive UV adhesives. Structural UV adhesives are used for bonding applications that require high strength and durability. Sealant adhesion UV adhesives are specifically designed for sealing and bonding applications, such as in construction and automotive industries. Pressure-sensitive UV adhesives offer temporary bonding solutions and are commonly used in industries like electronics and medical devices. Each type of UV adhesive serves specific purposes based on their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
