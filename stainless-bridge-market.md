<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Stainless Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional</li><li>Cantilever</li><li>Maryland</li><li>Implant-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless bridge market comprises various types catering to different dental needs. Traditional bridges involve crowns on adjacent teeth, providing support for a prosthetic tooth. Cantilever bridges extend from one anchor tooth, suitable for areas with only one adjacent tooth. Maryland bridges use a metal framework to support a false tooth, requiring minimal alteration of neighboring teeth. Implant-supported bridges involve anchoring the prosthetic teeth directly to dental implants, offering enhanced stability and longevity, making them ideal for edentulous patients.</p></p>
