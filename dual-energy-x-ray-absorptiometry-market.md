<p><ul><li>Axial Bone Densitometer</li><li>Peripheral Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual energy X ray absorptiometry (DXA) market consists of two main types: axial bone densitometer and peripheral bone densitometer. Axial bone densitometers are used to measure bone density at central skeletal sites, such as the spine and hip, and are considered the gold standard for diagnosing osteoporosis. Peripheral bone densitometers, on the other hand, are portable devices used to measure bone density at peripheral skeletal sites, such as the wrist and heel, providing a quick and convenient screening tool for assessing bone health.</p></p>
