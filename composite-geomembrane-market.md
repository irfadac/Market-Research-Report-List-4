<p><strong>The Composite Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDPE</li><li>LLDPE</li><li>PVC</li><li>FPP</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite geomembranes are made up of different types of materials, each offering unique characteristics and benefits. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) geomembranes provide excellent chemical resistance and durability. Linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) geomembranes offer flexibility and puncture resistance. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) geomembranes are known for their resistance to UV exposure. Flexible polypropylene (FPP) geomembranes are lightweight and resistant to environmental stress cracking. Other types of composite geomembranes may include materials such as ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) or chlorosulfonated polyethylene (CSPE), each with its own set of advantages.</p></p>
