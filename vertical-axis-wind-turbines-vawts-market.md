<p><p>Vertical-axis Wind Turbines (VAWTs) come in two main types in the market: Darrieus and Savonius. Darrieus turbines have a high efficiency and are commonly used in large-scale wind farms. They have a vertical rotating shaft with curved blades that capture wind from all directions. On the other hand, Savonius turbines have a simple design with S-shaped blades that are best suited for low wind speeds. They are commonly used for small-scale applications such as residential homes or off-grid systems.</p></p>
