<p><p>Gene editing market types include Zinc finger nucleases (ZFNs), Transcription Activator-Like Effector-based Nucleases (TALENs), and CRISPR-Cas system. ZFNs are protein-based nucleases that can be engineered to target specific DNA sequences. TALENs utilize customizable DNA-binding proteins to cut specific gene sequences. The CRISPR-Cas system is a revolutionary gene editing tool that uses RNA molecules to guide the Cas nuclease to specific DNA sequences for editing. These technologies are being widely adopted in research, biotechnology, and medical fields for precise gene modification.</p></p>
