<p><p>Electronic skin patches are wearable devices that monitor health parameters. There are three main types in the market:</p><p>1. Stretchable circuits: These patches contain flexible circuits that can bend and stretch without losing functionality, allowing for comfortable wear on moving body parts.</p><p>2. Stretchable conductors: These patches use conductive materials that can stretch along with the skin, maintaining connectivity and functionality even during movement.</p><p>3. Electro-active polymers: These patches contain polymers that change shape or properties in response to electrical stimulation, allowing for dynamic sensing and feedback capabilities.</p></p>
