On the other hand, Blended Fuel systems combine traditional fossil fuels with alternative sources like biodiesel or LNG to achieve a more efficient and environmentally friendly propulsion solution. Both types aim to provide a sustainable and cost-effective alternative to traditional marine engines, meeting the growing demand for cleaner maritime technology.</p></p>
