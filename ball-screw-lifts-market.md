<p><ul><li>Rotary Ball Screw</li><li>Translation Ball Screw</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screw lifts are categorized into three main types based on their motion mechanisms: rotary ball screw, translation ball screw, and others. Rotary ball screw lifts involve rotation of the screw to move the load vertically, while translation ball screw lifts move the load through linear motion of the screw. The "others" category may include variations of ball screw lifts that combine different motion mechanisms or utilize unique designs for specific applications. Each type offers distinct advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the lifting task.</p></p>
