<p><ul><li>Hypothermic Perfusion Preservation (HPP)</li><li>Static Cold Storage (SCS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organ preservation solutions are used to maintain the viability of organs before transplantation. Hypothermic Perfusion Preservation (HPP) involves circulating cold preservation solution through the organ to maintain optimal conditions. Static Cold Storage (SCS) involves immersing the organ in a cold preservation solution without circulation. Other preservation methods include normothermic perfusion and oxygenated hypothermic perfusion. These solutions help to extend the preservation time of organs outside the body, improving transplant success rates.</p></p>
