<p><p>Dental management software can be classified into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based systems. On-premise software requires the installation and maintenance of the system on the user's own servers, providing greater control and security but requiring more IT resources. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers, offering accessibility from any device with an internet connection, scalability, and automatic updates. Both types of software have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs and preferences of dental practices.</p></p>
