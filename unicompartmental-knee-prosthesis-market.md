<p><strong>The Unicompartmental Knee Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cemented Bone</li><li>Non-Cemented Bone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unicompartmental knee prosthesis come in two market types: cemented bone and non-cemented bone. Cemented bone prostheses are fixed into place using bone cement, providing immediate stability and support. Non-cemented bone prostheses rely on the natural bone to grow into the implant over time, allowing for a more natural integration. Each market type has its own benefits and considerations, with cemented bone being more stable initially and non-cemented bone offering potential for long-term bone growth and integration.</p></p>
