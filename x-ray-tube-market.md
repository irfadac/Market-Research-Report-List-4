<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray tube market primarily consists of two types: stationary anode X-ray tubes and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary anode tubes are typically used in applications requiring lower power and shorter exposure times, making them suitable for dental and some portable X-ray systems. In contrast, rotating anode tubes are designed for higher performance, allowing for greater heat dissipation and extended exposure periods, making them ideal for advanced medical imaging and diagnostic applications, such as computed tomography (CT) and radiography.</p></p>
