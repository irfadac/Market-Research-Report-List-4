<p><p>Mechanical pavers are designed with mechanical components such as gears and chains to drive the machine and spread material evenly on the surface. They are suitable for medium to large paving projects. Hydrostatic pavers, on the other hand, use hydraulic systems for smoother control and maneuverability. They are ideal for smaller projects or where precision is crucial. Both types of pavers are widely used in the construction industry for laying asphalt, concrete, or other materials on roads, parking lots, and other surfaces.</p></p>
