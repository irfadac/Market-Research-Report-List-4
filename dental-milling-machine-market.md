<p><p>Dental milling machines can be classified into two main types: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal milling machines have the spindle positioned horizontally, allowing for easier control of the cutting tool. Vertical milling machines have the spindle positioned vertically, allowing for more precise and detailed dental work. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the dental procedure. Both horizontal and vertical milling machines play a crucial role in the dental industry by providing precise and efficient milling capabilities.</p></p>
