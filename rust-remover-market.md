<p><p>Rust remover market can be categorized into three main types based on their chemical properties: Acidity Rust Remover, Alkaline Rust Remover, and Neutral Rust Remover. Acidity rust remover products contain acids like oxalic or phosphoric acid, which work by dissolving rust. On the other hand, alkaline rust removers use alkalis such as sodium hydroxide to break down rust. Neutral rust removers are a balanced blend of acidic and alkaline substances, making them versatile for various rust removal applications. Each type of rust remover offers unique advantages and is suited for different surfaces and conditions.</p></p>
