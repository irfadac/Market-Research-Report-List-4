<p><ul><li>Calender PVC Leather</li><li>Rotary Screen Coating PVC Leather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC leather can be classified into two main types based on the manufacturing process - Calender PVC leather and Rotary Screen Coating PVC leather. Calender PVC leather is produced by feeding a plasticized PVC compound through a series of heated rollers, while Rotary Screen Coating PVC leather involves applying a liquid PVC mixture onto a fabric base using a rotary screen printing technique. Both types of PVC leather offer different textures, designs, and properties to cater to various market needs and preferences.</p></p>
