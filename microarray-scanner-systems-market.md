<p><p>Microarray scanner systems are categorized into different types based on their technology and function. Fluorescence microarray scanners are designed to detect fluorescent signals emitted by labeled molecules on the microarray. Charge-coupled devices (CCD) microarray scanners use CCD technology to convert light signals into electronic signals for detection. Colorimetric microarray scanners are used to detect color changes on the microarray surface. Other types of microarray scanners include those that use different technologies or methods for signal detection and data analysis.</p></p>
