<p><p>Dental polymerization ovens are essential equipment in the dental industry for curing and hardening dental materials. Four main types of dental polymerization ovens are muffle, vacuum, infrared, and microwave ovens. Muffle ovens are traditional with an enclosed chamber for uniform heating. Vacuum ovens remove air during the curing process for better results. Infrared ovens use radiation for curing, while microwave ovens provide quick and efficient curing. Other types include combination ovens that offer multiple curing methods for different materials.</p></p>
