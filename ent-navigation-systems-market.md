<p><ul><li>Optical Navigation</li><li>Electromagnetic Navigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ENT navigation systems are essential tools used during minimally invasive surgeries in the field of otolaryngology. Optical navigation systems use cameras to track the position of surgical instruments in real time, providing accurate guidance during procedures. On the other hand, electromagnetic navigation systems utilize electromagnetic fields to track instrument movement. Both types of navigation systems offer precise guidance to surgeons, allowing for more efficient and successful surgeries in the delicate and complex anatomy of the ear, nose, and throat.</p></p>
