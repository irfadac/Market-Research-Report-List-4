<p><p>Portable scanners come in two main types - manual scanners and automatic scanners. Manual scanners require the user to physically move the device over the document or image being scanned. They are more suitable for smaller, irregularly shaped documents. Automatic scanners, on the other hand, can scan multiple pages at once without the need for manual intervention. They are more suited for large volumes of documents that need to be scanned quickly and efficiently. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the portable scanner market.</p></p>
