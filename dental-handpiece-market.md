<p><p>The dental handpiece market can be classified into three main types: air-driven handpieces, electric handpieces, and hybrid air-electric handpieces. Air-driven handpieces are powered by compressed air and are cost-effective, but may have higher noise levels. Electric handpieces are powered by electricity and offer more consistent performance with less noise. Hybrid air-electric handpieces combine the benefits of both air-driven and electric handpieces, providing a balance of power and efficiency for dental procedures. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements within the dental industry.</p></p>
