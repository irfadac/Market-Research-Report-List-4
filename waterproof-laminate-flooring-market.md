<p><p>Waterproof laminate flooring is available in two main types: Three Layer and Multilayer. Three Layer laminate flooring is made up of three layers - a wear layer, a decorative layer, and a core layer. Multilayer laminate flooring, on the other hand, consists of multiple layers that include a moisture-resistant backing layer, a core layer, a decorative layer, and a wear layer. Both types offer waterproof properties, making them ideal for areas prone to moisture like bathrooms and kitchens.</p></p>
