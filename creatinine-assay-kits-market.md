<p><p>Creatinine assay kits are used for measuring the levels of creatinine in biological samples. There are different types of kits available in the market including Jaffes Kinetic Test, Creatinine-PAP, and ELISA. Jaffes Kinetic Test is a commonly used method based on the measurement of color change as creatinine reacts with picrate under alkaline conditions. Creatinine-PAP assay kits utilize the enzyme creatininase in combination with creatinine amidohydrolase. ELISA kits use an enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay for the detection of creatinine levels in samples.</p></p>
