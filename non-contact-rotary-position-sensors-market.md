<p><p>Non-contact rotary position sensors are devices used to measure the angular position of a rotating object without physically contacting it. These sensors are available in two main types: digital output and analog output. Digital output sensors provide a discrete output signal indicating a specific position, while analog output sensors offer a continuous output signal proportional to the position of the object. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industrial applications such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics for accurate position monitoring and control.</p></p>
