<p><p>Sleep apnea devices market can be divided into two main types: therapeutic devices and diagnostic devices. Therapeutic devices include Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) machines, oral appliances, and airway clearance systems. These devices are designed to help individuals with sleep apnea breathe more easily during sleep. Diagnostic devices, on the other hand, are used to diagnose sleep apnea and its severity. These devices include polysomnography machines, home sleep testing kits, and oximeters. Both types of devices play a crucial role in managing sleep apnea and improving overall sleep quality.</p></p>
