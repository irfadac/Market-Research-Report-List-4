<p><p>Centrifugal Hair Dryer and Axial Hair Dryer are two main types of handhold hair dryers available in the market. Centrifugal hair dryers work by using a fan to create high-speed air flow, which helps dry hair quickly and efficiently. On the other hand, axial hair dryers utilize a propeller to generate airflow, resulting in a more gentle and less noisy drying experience. Both types of hair dryers have their own advantages and are suitable for different hair types and styling needs.</p></p>
