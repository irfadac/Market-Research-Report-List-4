<p><strong>The Optical Sight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Sight market can be categorized into two main types: Image Intensifier and Thermal Image. Image Intensifier sights utilize advanced technology to amplify ambient light, allowing the user to see better in low-light conditions. On the other hand, Thermal Image sights detect heat signatures emitted by objects, providing clear vision even in complete darkness. Both types of optical sights are popular choices for military, law enforcement, and civilian applications due to their enhanced visibility and accuracy capabilities.</p></p>
