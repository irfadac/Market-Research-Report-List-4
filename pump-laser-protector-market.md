<p><ul><li>Singlemode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pump Laser Protector market is categorized into two types: Singlemode and Multimode. Singlemode pump laser protectors are designed for use with singlemode fibers, ensuring efficient light transmission over long distances with minimal loss and optimal performance. Conversely, multimode pump laser protectors cater to multimode fibers, which allow multiple light paths for shorter distances, offering higher power handling and greater bandwidth. These distinctions help in selecting appropriate technologies for specific applications in fiber optic communication and laser systems.</p></p>
