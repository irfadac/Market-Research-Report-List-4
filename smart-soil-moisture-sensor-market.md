<p><strong>The Smart Soil Moisture Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensors</li><li>Soil Water Potential Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart soil moisture sensor market primarily comprises two types: volumetric soil moisture sensors and soil water potential sensors. Volumetric soil moisture sensors measure the amount of water contained in the soil by detecting changes in electrical properties, providing real-time data for irrigation management. Soil water potential sensors, on the other hand, measure the water potential in the soil, indicating how easily plants can access moisture. Both types contribute to enhanced agricultural efficiency and sustainable water management practices.</p></p>
