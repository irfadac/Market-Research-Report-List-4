<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electron Beam Resists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Electron Beam Resists</li><li>Negative Electron Beam Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron beam resists market is categorized into positive and negative electron beam resists. Positive electron beam resists become soluble when exposed to electron beams, allowing precise patterning for applications in semiconductors and nanotechnology. In contrast, negative electron beam resists harden upon exposure, resulting in insoluble patterns that enable high-resolution imaging and structural applications. Both types are essential for advanced lithography processes, offering unique properties that cater to evolving demands in microfabrication and material science.</p></p>
