<p><p>Manganese sulfate is available in three main forms in the market: powder, granular, and prill. Granular manganese sulfate is larger in size and slower to dissolve, making it suitable for soil applications. Prill manganese sulfate is in the form of tiny spherical pellets, allowing for easy handling and application. Each form has its unique characteristics and is used in different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
