<p><ul><li>Below 35KV</li><li>Between 35KV and 110KV</li><li>Above 110KV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transmission line arresters are categorized into three types based on the voltage levels they are designed for. Below 35KV arrester are utilized in low voltage transmission lines to protect against transient overvoltage. Between 35KV and 110KV arresters are designed for medium voltage transmission lines to prevent damage from lightning strikes and switching surges. Above 110KV arresters are used in high voltage transmission lines to safeguard equipment from electrical disturbances. Each type of arrester provides specific protection tailored to the voltage levels of the transmission lines.</p></p>
