<p><p>CPA liability insurance covers two main types of risks: Directors & Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects board members and officers from lawsuits alleging wrongful acts in their role, such as mismanagement or breach of fiduciary duty. E&O insurance, on the other hand, protects professionals from claims of negligence or mistakes in services provided. Both types of insurance are crucial for CPAs to safeguard their personal assets and reputations in the face of potential legal action.</p></p>
