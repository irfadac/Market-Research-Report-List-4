<p><strong>The Automatic Bakery Packaging Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li><li>Tertiary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Primary packaging refers to the packaging immediately in contact with the bakery product, such as a bag or box. Secondary packaging involves grouping primary packages together for easier handling and transportation, like placing several bags in a larger carton. Tertiary packaging is the outermost packaging that facilitates distribution and protects multiple units, such as pallets or shrink wrap. Automatic bakery packaging machines cater to all three types of packaging, providing efficiency and convenience in the packaging process.</p></p>
