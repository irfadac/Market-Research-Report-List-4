<p><p>Natural surgical glue market consists of three main types: fibrin, collagen, and gelatin. Fibrin glue is made from blood plasma proteins and is commonly used in surgeries to promote healing and tissue sealing. Collagen-based glues are derived from animal connective tissue and are known for their biocompatibility and strength. Gelatin-based glues are also derived from animal sources and are utilized in various surgical procedures for wound closure. Each type of glue offers unique properties and benefits for medical applications.</p></p>
