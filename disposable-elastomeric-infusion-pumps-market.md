<p><p>Disposable elastomeric infusion pumps market can be categorized into two types: continuous rate elastomeric pumps and variable rate elastomeric pumps. Continuous rate elastomeric pumps deliver medication at a consistent rate over a set period of time, making them suitable for long-term treatments. On the other hand, variable rate elastomeric pumps allow for adjustable medication delivery rates, providing more flexibility in dosing regimens. Both types of pumps are commonly used in healthcare settings for delivering medications such as antibiotics, analgesics, and chemotherapy drugs.</p></p>
