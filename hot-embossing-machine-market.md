<p><p>Hot embossing machines are classified into three types based on their automation level: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines are highly automated and require minimal human intervention, making them suitable for high-volume production. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input but still offer efficiency and precision. Manual machines are operated entirely by hand and are typically used for small-scale production or prototyping. Each type of hot embossing machine caters to different production needs and budgets.</p></p>
