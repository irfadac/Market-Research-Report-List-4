<p><p>The LED encapsulation market consists of three primary types: epoxy, silicone, and polyurethane. Epoxy encapsulants offer excellent adhesion and thermal stability, making them suitable for various applications. Silicone encapsulants provide flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures, ideal for outdoor and harsh environments. Polyurethane encapsulants combine durability with moisture resistance, making them effective for industrial applications. Each type enhances LED performance by protecting components from environmental factors, ensuring longevity and reliability in diverse settings.</p></p>
