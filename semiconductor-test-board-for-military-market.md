<p><p>The semiconductor test board market for military applications encompasses various types of boards designed for testing and validation. Probe cards are used for high-frequency testing of individual chips on wafers, ensuring functionality before packaging. Load boards evaluate assembled devices by simulating operational conditions, verifying performance under load. Burn-in boards (BIB) facilitate accelerated aging tests by subjecting devices to stress conditions to identify early failures. Each board type plays a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and durability of military semiconductor components.</p></p>
