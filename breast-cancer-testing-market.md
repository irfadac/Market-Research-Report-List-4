Screening Tests are used to detect the presence of cancer in asymptomatic individuals, typically through mammograms. Diagnostic Tests are performed after a screening test to confirm the presence of cancer and determine its characteristics. Monitoring Tests are used to track the progression of the disease and the effectiveness of treatment over time.
