<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Detached</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial compactors can be classified into two main types: fixed compactors and detached compactors. Fixed compactors are permanently attached to a building or structure, typically used for larger volumes of waste disposal. Detached compactors, on the other hand, are standalone units that can be moved around and placed at different locations as needed. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks, with fixed compactors offering convenience and efficiency for high-volume waste management, while detached compactors provide flexibility and easy relocation options.</p></p>
