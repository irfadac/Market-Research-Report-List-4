<p><strong>The Contrast Medium Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-head Contrast Injector</li><li>Dual-head Contrast Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-head Contrast Injector and Dual-head Contrast Injector are two main types of Contrast Medium Injectors used in medical imaging. Single-head injectors have one syringe and are used for injecting contrast medium into the patient's bloodstream. On the other hand, Dual-head injectors have two syringes, allowing for simultaneous injection of contrast medium into multiple sites. Single-head injectors are typically used for simple imaging procedures, while Dual-head injectors are used for more complex imaging procedures requiring contrast medium in multiple areas.</p></p>
