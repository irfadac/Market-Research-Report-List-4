<p><p>Non-oriented cold rolled electrical steel is classified into two main market types: fully processed and semi-processed. Fully processed steel undergoes extensive manufacturing processes including annealing, coating, and cutting, resulting in a final product ready for use in electrical applications. On the other hand, semi-processed steel requires additional processing by the end-user such as trimming, insulation coating, and punching before it can be utilized effectively in electrical components. Both types cater to different industrial needs and offer varying levels of convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
