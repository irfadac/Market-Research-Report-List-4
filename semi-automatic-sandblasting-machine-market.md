<p><p>Semi-automatic sandblasting machines are available in two main types: dry and wet. In the dry market, abrasive material is propelled at a surface using compressed air, resulting in effective cleaning and roughening. This type is commonly used for tasks like rust removal and surface preparation. In the wet market, water is used in conjunction with the abrasive material to reduce dust and control the spread of contaminants. Wet sandblasting is ideal for delicate surfaces and projects that require minimal dust production.</p></p>
