<p><p>Benchtop fiber optic test equipment includes optical power and loss meters, optical spectrum analyzers, optical time domain reflectometers, and other test equipment. Optical power and loss meters measure the power levels and losses in fiber optic systems. Optical spectrum analyzers analyze the spectral characteristics of the light in fiber optic networks. Optical time domain reflectometers measure the time taken for light signals to reflect back in fiber optic cables. Other test equipment includes tools for testing insertion loss, return loss, and fiber optic connectors.</p></p>
