<p><p>The fuel management system market can be divided into two main types: card-based systems and on-site systems. Card-based systems involve the use of fuel cards that employees can use to purchase fuel at approved gas stations. On the other hand, on-site systems involve the installation of fuel tanks and pumps directly on the company's premises for refueling vehicles internally. Both types of systems help companies track and manage fuel usage, control costs, and improve efficiency in managing their fuel supply.</p></p>
