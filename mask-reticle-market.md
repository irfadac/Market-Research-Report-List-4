It is an essential component of the photolithography process, allowing for the precise replication of intricate patterns on a silicon wafer.</p><p>The mask reticle market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.00% during the forecast period. The growth of this market can be attributed to the increasing demand for smaller and more powerful electronic devices, driving the need for higher resolution and more advanced mask reticles.
