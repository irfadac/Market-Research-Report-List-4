<p><p>Angiography Equipment Market consists of three main types: X-Ray Angiography, CT Angiography, and MR Angiography. </p><p>X-Ray Angiography uses X-ray technology to produce detailed images of blood vessels. CT Angiography involves the use of a CT scanner to create highly detailed images of blood vessels throughout the body. MR Angiography uses magnetic resonance imaging technology to produce high-resolution images of blood vessels without the need for ionizing radiation. These different types of angiography equipment cater to various clinical needs and preferences in diagnosing and treating vascular conditions.</p></p>
