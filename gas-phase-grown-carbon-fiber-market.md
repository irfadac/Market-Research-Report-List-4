<p><p>Gas phase grown carbon fiber market can be categorized into PAN based and Asphalt based types. PAN based carbon fibers are produced from polyacrylonitrile fibers that are heat-treated in an inert atmosphere to result in high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, Asphalt based carbon fibers are derived from petroleum asphalt and possess unique properties suited for specific applications. Both types have excellent mechanical properties and heat resistance, making them ideal for use in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods.</p></p>
