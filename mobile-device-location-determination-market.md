<p><p>Mobile-device location determination can be categorized into various market types. Global Positioning System (GPS) relies on satellite signals to pinpoint the exact location of a device. Direction based geolocation determines location based on the direction the device is facing. Distance based positioning calculates location by measuring the distance from known reference points. Each market type offers unique advantages and may be more suitable for different applications depending on the level of accuracy needed.</p></p>
