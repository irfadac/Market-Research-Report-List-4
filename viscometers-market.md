<p><p>Viscometers are devices used to measure the viscosity of fluids. There are three main types of viscometers on the market: in-line process viscometers, portable viscometers, and laboratory viscometers. In-line process viscometers are installed directly into a process pipeline to monitor viscosity continuously. Portable viscometers are handheld devices that are easy to carry around and can be used in various locations. Laboratory viscometers are more sophisticated instruments used in research and development settings for precise viscosity measurements.</p></p>
