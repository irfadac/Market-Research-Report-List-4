<p><p>Fatigue machines are used to simulate fatigue loading on materials for testing purposes. There are three main types of fatigue machines based on the frequency of loading: low frequency fatigue machines apply loading at a slow rate, medium frequency fatigue machines apply loading at a moderate rate, and high frequency fatigue machines apply loading at a fast rate. Each type of fatigue machine is designed to assess the durability and performance of materials under different loading conditions, helping manufacturers ensure product reliability and safety.</p></p>
