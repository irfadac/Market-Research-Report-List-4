<p><p>The sounding rocket market is categorized into two primary types: one-stage and multistage sounding rockets. One-stage sounding rockets are designed for simpler missions, typically reaching altitudes of 30-100 km, ideal for atmospheric studies and technology demonstrations. In contrast, multistage sounding rockets consist of multiple propulsion stages, enabling higher altitudes, often exceeding 200 km. They are utilized for advanced research applications, such as space science and upper-atmosphere exploration, offering more versatility and payload capacity for complex experimental missions.</p></p>
