<p><strong>The Internet Protocol Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized Internet Protocol Camera</li><li>Decentralized Internet Protocol Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized Internet Protocol Cameras are a type of IP camera that requires a central network video recorder (NVR) to store and manage recordings. These cameras are typically used in larger video surveillance systems where multiple cameras are installed. Decentralized Internet Protocol Cameras, on the other hand, are standalone devices that have built-in recording capabilities and can store footage locally on a memory card or network-attached storage (NAS) device. These cameras are usually easier to install and maintain, making them ideal for smaller-scale surveillance systems.</p></p>
