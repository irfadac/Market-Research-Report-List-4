<p><p>The insulated safety glass market is primarily segmented into three types: double-deck, triple-deck, and others. Double-deck insulated glass consists of two layers of glass with a space in between, providing effective thermal insulation and soundproofing. Triple-deck glass adds an additional layer for enhanced insulation and energy efficiency, ideal for extreme climates. The "others" category encompasses specialized configurations and variations, such as laminated or coated glass, catering to specific aesthetic or functional requirements in diverse applications.</p></p>
