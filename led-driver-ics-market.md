Multi-channel drivers offer flexible control over multiple LED strings, enabling advanced color mixing and dimming capabilities for complex lighting setups. "Others" embrace specialized drivers used in niche applications, such as automotive lighting or horticultural systems, addressing unique performance requirements across various markets.</p></p>
