<p><strong>The Lychee Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extracted Honey</li><li>Pressed Honey</li><li>Comb Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lychee honey market is categorized into three main types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is produced by mechanically separating honey from the comb using a centrifugal force, resulting in a clear, liquid product. Pressed honey is derived from squeezing the honey out of the comb, retaining some natural wax. Comb honey consists of honey still sealed in its natural beeswax structure, offering a unique presentation and flavor experience. Each type caters to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
