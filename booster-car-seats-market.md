<p><p>Booster car seats are designed to provide extra protection for children who have outgrown their forward-facing convertible car seats. There are three main types of booster seats on the market: backless booster, high back booster, and combination seat. Backless boosters are a simple cushion that lifts the child up to the correct height for the seat belt. High back boosters offer additional head and neck support. Combination seats can be used as a harness booster seat and then converted to a belt-positioning booster as the child grows.</p></p>
