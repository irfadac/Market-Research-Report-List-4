<p><strong>The Die Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die casting machine market can be segmented into two types: hot chamber die casting machines and cold chamber die casting machines. Hot chamber die casting machines are used for zinc, magnesium, and other low melting point alloys, as the molten metal is contained within the machine itself. Cold chamber die casting machines are used for high melting point alloys such as aluminum and brass, as the molten metal is poured into the machine from an external furnace. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the die casting industry.</p></p>
