<p><p>The mobile portable printers market primarily includes three types: inkjet printers, laser printers, and thermal printers. Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink to produce high-quality images and are versatile for various printing needs. Laser printers, known for their speed and efficiency, use toner to deliver crisp text and quality graphics, making them ideal for high-volume printing. Thermal printers rely on heat to transfer ink onto paper, commonly used for labels and receipts due to their durability and speed.</p></p>
