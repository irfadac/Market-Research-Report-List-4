<p><p>Lithium-ion cell and battery pack markets primarily feature two configurations: series and parallel battery packs. A series battery pack connects multiple cells in a single chain, which increases the overall voltage while keeping the capacity the same, ideal for applications requiring higher voltage. Conversely, parallel battery packs connect cells side by side, maintaining voltage but boosting capacity, allowing for longer usage times. These configurations cater to varying power and energy needs across sectors like consumer electronics, electric vehicles, and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
