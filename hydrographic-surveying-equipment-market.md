Side scan sonars create detailed images of the seafloor using sound waves. Other tools in the market include multibeam echosounders, magnetometers, and underwater cameras. These equipment types are crucial for a wide range of applications such as maritime navigation, dredging operations, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
