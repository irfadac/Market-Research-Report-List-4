<p><strong>The Tricone Drill Bits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCI Drill Bits</li><li>Milled Tooth Drill Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tricone drill bits are commonly used in drilling operations and come in two main types: TCI (Tungsten Carbide Insert) drill bits and Milled Tooth drill bits. TCI drill bits are equipped with durable tungsten carbide inserts that provide excellent resistance to wear and abrasion, making them ideal for hard rock formations. In contrast, Milled Tooth drill bits have steel teeth that are milled into the cones, offering better performance in softer formations. Both types cater to different drilling needs and geological conditions in the market.</p></p>
