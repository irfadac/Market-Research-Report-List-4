<p><p>Sterile surgical yarn is categorized into two main types - Sterile Absorbable Surgical Yarns and Sterile Nonabsorbable Surgical Yarns. Sterile Absorbable Surgical Yarns are designed to be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal post-surgery. On the other hand, Sterile Nonabsorbable Surgical Yarns are made from materials that do not break down in the body and need to be removed manually after the healing process is complete. These two types cater to different surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
