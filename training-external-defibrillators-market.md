<p><p>Training external defibrillators market includes automatic and manual types. Automatic external defibrillators deliver a shock to the heart without manual intervention, making them easier to use for individuals without medical training. Manual external defibrillators require trained professionals to analyze the patient's heart rhythm and deliver the shock manually. Both types are essential for training purposes to simulate real-life scenarios and practice proper usage and technique in emergency situations. This diverse market caters to various training needs in healthcare and other industries.</p></p>
