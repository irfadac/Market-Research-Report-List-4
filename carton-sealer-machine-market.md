<p><p>Carton sealer machines are available in three main types based on their operation and level of automation: Automatic, Semi-Automatic, and Manual. Automatic carton sealers are fully automated machines that can seal multiple boxes quickly and efficiently. Semi-Automatic carton sealers require some manual assistance in feeding and adjusting the boxes before sealing. Manual carton sealers are operated entirely by hand, requiring the operator to position the box and manually apply the tape. Each type of carton sealer machine caters to different production needs and levels of automation.</p></p>
