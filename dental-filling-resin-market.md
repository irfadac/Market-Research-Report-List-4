<p><ul><li>Composite Fillings</li><li>Amalgam Fillings</li><li>Glass Ionomer Fillings</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental filling resin market comprises various types, each with unique properties. Composite fillings are tooth-colored resins offering aesthetic appeal and versatility, making them popular for front teeth. Amalgam fillings, made from a blend of metals, are durable and cost-effective, suited for back teeth. Glass ionomer fillings release fluoride, providing added protection against decay and bond chemically with the tooth structure, making them ideal for areas needing additional support. Other materials include resin ionomers and temporary fillings, catering to diverse dental needs.</p></p>
