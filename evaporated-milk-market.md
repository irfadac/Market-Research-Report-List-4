<p><p>The evaporated milk market comprises primarily two types: skimmed and whole evaporated milk. Skimmed evaporated milk is produced by removing most of the fat content, making it a lower-calorie option for health-conscious consumers. On the other hand, whole evaporated milk retains its fat, offering a richer flavor and creamier texture, appealing to those seeking a more indulgent product. Both types are valued for their long shelf life and versatility in cooking and baking applications.</p></p>
