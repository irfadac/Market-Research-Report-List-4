<p><p>Single-phase analyzers are devices used to analyze and measure electrical power in a single-phase system. There are two main types of single-phase analyzers: portable phase analyzers and benchtop phase analyzers. Portable phase analyzers are compact and convenient for on-the-go measurements, making them ideal for field testing and troubleshooting. Benchtop phase analyzers are more stationary and designed for more in-depth analysis in a laboratory or industrial setting. Both types of analyzers play essential roles in ensuring the efficient operation of single-phase electrical systems.</p></p>
