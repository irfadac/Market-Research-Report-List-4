<p><strong>The Cooking Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instant-Read</li><li>In-Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cooking Thermometer market consists of two main types: Instant-Read and In-Probe thermometers. Instant-Read thermometers provide quick and accurate temperature readings by simply inserting the probe into the food. These are ideal for checking the temperature of meats, soups, and other dishes. In-Probe thermometers are designed to be inserted into the food during the cooking process, providing continuous temperature monitoring. This type is commonly used for roasting or grilling meats to ensure they are cooked to the desired doneness.</p></p>
