<p><ul><li>Sealers</li><li>Micro-sealers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary dental laboratory sealers are equipment used to create a strong seal between dental materials during the fabrication process. Sealers are larger machines used for sealing larger quantities of materials, while micro-sealers are smaller, more precise machines designed for sealing small or delicate items. Both types of sealers help ensure the quality and longevity of dental prosthetics by securely sealing materials together. The choice between sealers and micro-sealers depends on the size and type of materials being used in the dental laboratory.</p></p>
