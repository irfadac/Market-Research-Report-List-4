<p><p>Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices market types include embolization coils, balloon and stent-assisted coils, access and delivery devices, aneurysm clips, and flow diversion devices. Embolization coils are used to block blood flow to an aneurysm, while balloon and stent-assisted coils provide support and stability during the procedure. Access and delivery devices help navigate the catheter to the aneurysm site, and aneurysm clips are used to seal off blood vessels. Flow diversion devices redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm to prevent rupture.</p></p>
