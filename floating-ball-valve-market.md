<p><p>A floating ball valve is a type of quarter-turn valve that utilizes a hollow, floating ball to control the flow of a fluid through a pipeline. The ball in the valve is free to move within the valve body, allowing it to pivot and seal against the valve seat. This design creates a tight seal when the valve is closed, preventing leakage of the fluid.</p><p>The Floating Ball Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to the increasing demand for floating ball valves in industries such as oil and gas, water and wastewater, and chemical processing.
