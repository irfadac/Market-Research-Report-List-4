<p><strong>The Belt Protection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Protection</li><li>Dynamic Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt Protection System is divided into two main types: Static Protection and Dynamic Protection. Static Protection involves systems that prevent belt damage during normal operation, such as tracking sensors and alignment devices. Dynamic Protection, on the other hand, includes more advanced systems that respond to sudden deviations or potential hazards in real-time, like rip detection sensors and belt misalignment alerts. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of conveyor belt systems.</p></p>
