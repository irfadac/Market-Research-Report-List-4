<p><p>Silicone elastic gaskets come in various types, including Saddle and Waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets are designed with a curved shape that resembles a saddle, providing a secure seal for applications requiring flexibility. On the other hand, Waveform gaskets feature a wavy pattern that allows for compression and expansion, making them ideal for situations where movement and vibration may occur. Both types of gaskets offer durability, resistance to high temperatures, and excellent sealing capabilities for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
