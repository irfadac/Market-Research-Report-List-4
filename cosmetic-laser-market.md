<p><p>Cosmetic laser market can be divided into two main types: ablative and non-ablative. Ablative lasers work by removing outer layers of skin to promote collagen production and improve skin texture, targeting deep wrinkles, scars, and pigmentation. Non-ablative lasers, on the other hand, work by heating the tissue beneath the skin's surface without damaging the outer layer, stimulating collagen production and smoothing out fine lines and wrinkles. Both types of lasers are used in various cosmetic procedures to rejuvenate and improve the appearance of the skin.</p></p>
