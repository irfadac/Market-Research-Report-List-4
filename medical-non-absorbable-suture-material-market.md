<p><p>Medical non-absorbable suture material market is divided into two main types: synthetic material and natural material. Synthetic materials are typically made from polymers such as nylon, polypropylene, or polyester, offering high tensile strength and minimal tissue reactivity. Natural materials, on the other hand, are derived from sources like silk, catgut, or collagen, providing good handling characteristics and biocompatibility. Both types cater to different surgical needs and preferences, offering a wide range of options for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
