<p><p>Personal Radiation Monitors are essential devices used to keep track of an individual's exposure to ionizing radiation. There are two main types of Personal Radiation Monitors - Active Type and Passive Type. </p><p>Active Type monitors continuously measure radiation levels in real-time and provide instant feedback to the wearer. In contrast, Passive Type monitors do not provide real-time readings but collect data over a certain period for later analysis. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of individuals working in radiation-exposed environments.</p></p>
