<p><p>Industrial plastic 3D printers utilize various technologies such as Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) to create complex plastic components with high accuracy. SLM involves using a high-power laser to selectively melt and fuse plastic powder layers, while EBM uses an electron beam to melt and solidify the plastic material. Other types of industrial plastic 3D printing technologies include Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Fused Filament Fabrication (FFF), each offering unique advantages and applications in manufacturing and prototyping processes.</p></p>
