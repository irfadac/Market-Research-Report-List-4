<p><p>Bioabsorbable sutures are surgical materials made from natural or synthetic polymers that can be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. The growing demand for minimally invasive surgeries and the preference for absorbable materials in wound closure are driving the bioabsorbable sutures market forward.</p><p>The market is anticipated to witness significant growth, expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.80% during the forecast period. Companies are focusing on R&D to create novel bioabsorbable materials, further propelling market growth.
