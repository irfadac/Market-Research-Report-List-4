<p><p>Poultry insurance consists of two main types: non-commercial mortality insurance and commercial mortality insurance. Non-commercial mortality insurance is typically used by small-scale poultry farmers to protect against losses from sudden deaths of their birds. Commercial mortality insurance is purchased by larger poultry operations to cover the financial impact of significant mortality events within their flocks. Both types of insurance help poultry farmers mitigate risks and safeguard their investments in the event of unforeseen circumstances.</p></p>
