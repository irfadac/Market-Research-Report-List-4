<p><p>Inline samplers are devices used to collect samples from a process stream for analysis. There are two main types of inline samplers on the market: manual and pneumatic. Manual inline samplers require a person to physically collect the sample from the process stream, while pneumatic inline samplers use pneumatic pressure to automatically extract and transfer samples. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, with manual samplers being more hands-on and pneumatic samplers being more automated and efficient.</p></p>
