<p><p>Film irrigation is a type of micro water spraying fertilizer integrated machine market that involves laying a thin plastic film over the soil to create a barrier between the soil and the atmosphere. Under film irrigation is a subcategory of film irrigation where water and fertilizer are delivered directly underneath the plastic film, allowing for more efficient use of resources and reducing water wastage. This method helps to maintain soil moisture, control weed growth, and deliver nutrients directly to the root zone of plants.</p></p>
