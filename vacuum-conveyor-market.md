<p><p>Vacuum conveyors are classified into two main market types: Integral Type and Split Type. Integral Type vacuum conveyors consist of a single, compact unit where the pump and conveyor are integrated together, providing a simple and space-saving solution. On the other hand, Split Type vacuum conveyors have separate units for the pump and conveyor, offering more flexibility in terms of installation and customization. Both types of vacuum conveyors are used in various industries for conveying materials efficiently and hygienically.</p></p>
