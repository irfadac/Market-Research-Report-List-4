<p><p>The Heli-Coil Thread Inserts market is primarily divided into two types: tanged and tangless thread inserts. Tanged thread inserts come with tangs that assist in installation and removal, making them suitable for applications requiring great torque strength. Conversely, tangless thread inserts are designed to provide a smoother finish and eliminate potential interference from tangs, making them ideal for tighter spaces and applications where aesthetics matter. Both types enhance the durability and functionality of threaded connections in various industries.</p></p>
