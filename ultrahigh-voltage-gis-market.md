Isolated Phase GIS separates each phase in its own compartment, while Integrated 3 Phase GIS combines all three phases in a single compartment. Hybrid GIS incorporates both air and gas insulation in its design. The 'Others' category comprises alternative designs that may not fit into the aforementioned types, offering a variety of options for ultrahigh voltage GIS solutions.</p></p>
