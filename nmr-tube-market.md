Plastic NMR tubes are the most cost-effective option and are suitable for routine applications. Quartz NMR tubes are used for high-resolution experiments due to their high purity and low chemical interference. Borosilicate glass NMR tubes are a popular choice for general laboratory use, offering good chemical resistance and durability. Each type of NMR tube caters to different needs and preferences within the scientific community.</p></p>
