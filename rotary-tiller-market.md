<p><p>Rotary tillers are used in gardening and agriculture for breaking and aerating soil. There are two main types of rotary tillers in the market: front-tine and rear-tine. Front-tine tillers have their rotating blades in front of the machine, making them easier to maneuver and suitable for smaller areas. Rear-tine tillers have blades located at the back, providing more power and stability for handling tough and compacted soil. Both types come in various sizes and designs to cater to different gardening and farming needs.</p></p>
