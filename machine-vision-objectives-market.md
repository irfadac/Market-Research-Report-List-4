<p><strong>The Machine Vision Objectives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Objective</li><li>Aspheric Objective</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision objectives are essential components in machine vision systems, serving as lenses that focus light onto sensors for image capture and analysis. Spherical objectives are traditional curved lenses that offer excellent image quality but are limited in correcting optical aberrations. Aspheric objectives, on the other hand, use non-spherical surfaces to correct aberrations, leading to sharper images and improved resolution. Both types cater to different market needs, with spherical objectives being more cost-effective while aspheric objectives are preferred for high precision applications.</p></p>
