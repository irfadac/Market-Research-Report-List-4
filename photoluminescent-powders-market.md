<p><p>Photoluminescent powders are available in two main market types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based photoluminescent powders are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and suitable for use on various surfaces such as textiles, paints, and coatings. Solvent-based photoluminescent powders are preferred for applications that require high durability and resistance to chemicals and weathering. Both types of photoluminescent powders offer glowing properties in the dark, making them ideal for safety-related products and materials.</p></p>
